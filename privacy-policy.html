Privacy Policy for Elva & Damian Finances

Effective date: [March 30, 2026]

Elva & Damian Finances (“the app,” “we,” “our”) is a private family finance app intended only for 2 users: Husband and Wife.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile information, such as name, bio, and profile picture
  • Login PIN information for account access
  • Financial records, including income, expenses, categories, dates, amounts, and related notes
  • Chat messages exchanged inside the app
  • App settings, such as selected currency, exchange rate, notification preferences, and sync status
  • Technical sync data, such as timestamps for last sync and pending sync items

2. How We Use Information

  • Let the 2 authorized users log in and use the app

  • Show profile information and chat messages
  • Apply selected settings, including currency and notifications

3. Where Data Is Stored

App data is stored in Supabase for cloud synchronization and locally on the user’s device for faster loading and offline display of previously synced content.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share app data with third parties for advertising. Data is processed only through services required to run the app, such as Supabase for database and file storage.

5. Data Retention

Data remains stored until it is edited or deleted by the user, or removed by the developer upon request. Locally cached data may remain on the device until the app is cleared, data is removed, or the app is uninstalled.

6. Deletion of Data

Users can delete certain content directly inside the app where supported. Deleted items are removed from Supabase immediately when deletion succeeds. To request full data removal, contact us using the details below.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data. However, no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for public use by children. It is a private family financial app.
